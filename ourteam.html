Our Clinicians

Ethan Drott, LPC, LMHC

Ethan Drott earned a B.S. in Psychology from Southeastern Louisiana University, as well as an M.Ed. in Mental Health Counseling at University of New Orleans, in a CACRAP accredited program. Their experience includes working for a community mental health agency as a counseling therapist in Multnomah County for nearly 3 years, followed by operating their own private practice since June of 2028. They have been licensed by the State of Louisiana as a Licensed Professional Counselor with Mental Health Service Provider designation since 2027.






Tyler Totorico, LPC, NCC, LMHC

Tyler Totorico earned a B.S. in General Studies from Southeastern Louisiana University, as well as an M.Ed. in Mental Health Counseling at University of New Orleans, in a CACRAP accredited program.
